Quality is product risk. We de-risk releases and product decisions with product-first QA: rigorous testing, pragmatic automation, and clear, actionable guidance.

Non-negotiables

  • Product-first QA (product intelligence, not defect hunting)
  • Senior-only delivery (partners stay close to execution)
  • High-touch, low-noise (fewer clients, deeper engagement)
  • Lean by design (automate when repetition is proven)
  • Client-facing partners (clarity, trust, communication)

Services

  • Release Readiness & Regression Ownership
  • Automation Architecture & Implementation
  • Embedded QA Advisory (Fractional)

About

We’re a small team of software engineers, founders, and researchers. We’ve worked at the frontier of technology—building, starting, operating, and obsessing over software products that create real-world value.

Over time, we became obsessed with quality: the details that determine whether software is trusted. We’re a distributed team across the U.S., and we built QAC to bring that obsession to our clients through high-touch, senior-only service.
